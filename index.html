About Me

Hello! I'm Minio Franz, a full-stack developer with several years of hands-on experience in programming and web development. I specialize in both frontend and backend technologies, building complete and functional web applications from the ground up.

I started my journey into programming when I was 17 years old, during my senior high school years. Since then, I've been consistently practicing and working on real-world projects, which allowed me to build a strong foundation in full-stack development. I've developed different systems and websites, often helping students with their research projects, and these experiences gave me practical skills beyond what is taught in the classroom.

Currently, I'm pursuing my Bachelor of Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology (BindTech-CT) at Cebu Technological University. Even while studying, I have taken the initiative to deepen my expertise by continuously learning modern technologies and applying them to real projects. I believe that being a great developer means being a lifelong learner, so I always make sure to update my knowledge and improve my coding practices.

My full-stack skills include working with frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks, as well as backend development using server-side languages, APIs, and databases. I also use AI tools to assist me in coding and research, helping me boost my productivity and creativity, but I ensure that critical thinking and solid understanding remain at the core of my work.

What sets me apart is my passion for technology, my strong work ethic, and my ability to learn independently. I'm driven by a genuine love for solving problems and creating solutions that make life easier for users. I'm excited to bring my skills, energy, and growing experience into opportunities where I can contribute meaningfully and continue to grow.
